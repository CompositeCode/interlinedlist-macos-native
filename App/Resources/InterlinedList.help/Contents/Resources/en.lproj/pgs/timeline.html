Reading the timeline

The timeline is the default view when you sign in. Select Timeline in the sidebar to return to it any time.

Switching feed scopes

Use the scope picker at the top of the timeline to choose what you see:

  • All — every public message visible to you.
  • Mine — messages you have posted.

The Following scope is coming in a future update.

Filtering by tag

Type a tag into the tag filter to narrow the feed to messages carrying that tag. Clear the field to return to the unfiltered feed.

Loading more

The timeline loads more messages automatically as you scroll near the end. To force a refresh, pull down at the top of the list.

Opening a thread

Click any message to open its thread in the detail pane. You see the original message and all its replies in order. The detail view is read-only in this release.

Coming in a future update

  • Inline replies, "I Dig!" reactions, and reposts from inside the thread view.
  • Scheduled posts and a dedicated Scheduled sidebar section.
  • Media attachments displayed inline.

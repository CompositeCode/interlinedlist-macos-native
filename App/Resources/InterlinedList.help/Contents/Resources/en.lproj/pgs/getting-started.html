Before you sign in

InterlinedList for macOS connects to your existing account at interlinedlist.com. If you don't have one yet, you can create it from the welcome window with Create account, or sign up on the website first.

Sign in

  1. Launch InterlinedList. The welcome window asks for your email and password.
  2. Enter your credentials and click Sign in.
  3. The app stores your access token in the macOS Keychain. You stay signed in between launches.
Note. If you forget your password, click Reset password on the welcome window and follow the email link.

The main window

The main window uses the standard macOS three-pane layout:

  • Sidebar — switch between Timeline, Lists, Profile, and other sections.
  • Content list — the items in the selected section.
  • Detail / inspector — the selected item's full content.

What you can do right now

  • Read your timeline. See Reading the timeline.
  • Open a message thread to see its replies.
  • Browse any user's public lists by username. See Browsing public lists.
  • View a basic profile header for any user who has at least one public message. See Profiles.

Coming in a future update

  • Creating and editing your own lists.
  • Documents with offline editing and sync.
  • Notifications, organizations, scheduled posts, media attachments, and CSV exports.
